# Retrieval Improvement Recommendations

This document outlines and ranks the proposed improvements to the Neurolens retrieval engine based on the evidence gathered from the 32 benchmark failure cases. The recommendations are prioritized by their expected benchmark impact, engineering effort, and associated risks.

## Ranked Recommendations

| Recommendation | Expected Gain | Effort | Risk | Evidence |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :--- |
| **1. Expand Hinglish Transliteration Glossary / Translate Roman-Script Hindi** <br> Enrich the current transliteration glossary (currently only 8 words) in `ai_service.py` to map common Hindi observation terms to English equivalents. | **High** <br> (+15.0% to +18.0% Top-3 Accuracy) | **Low to Medium** <br> (Expanding lookup dictionary or loading from JSON) | **Low** <br> (No impact on pure English inputs) | **26 out of 32 failure cases (81.25%)** are caused by transliterated Hinglish words that the multilingual model cannot align with English milestones (e.g. Failure Cases 1, 3, 4, 6, 7, 8, etc.). |
| **2. Enrich Milestone Metadata with Concrete Examples (Semantic Enrichment)** <br> Add concrete examples (e.g., "holding phone to ear", "opening box lids") directly into the `description` field of the milestones in the seed database. | **Moderate** <br> (+4.0% to +6.0% Top-3 Accuracy) | **Low** <br> (Content modification only; no code changes) | **Low** <br> (Enhances search vocabulary without side effects) | **Failure Case 23** ("toy receiver to her ear" missed "intended purpose" milestone) and **Failure Case 32** ("opened box lid" missed "opens lids" milestone). |
| **3. Implement Soft Domain Boosting** <br> Run the existing `retrieve_domains` function first to detect the top domain, and apply a small weight boost (e.g., 1.1x) to milestones matching that domain during retrieval. | **Moderate** <br> (+3.0% to +5.0% Top-3 Accuracy) | **Medium** <br> (Minor changes to ranking logic in `retrieve_milestones`) | **Medium** <br> (May penalize correct milestones if domain prediction is wrong) | **12 cases of mutual confusion** between Communication and Social Emotional domains (e.g. Failure Cases 5, 9, 16, 18, 19). |
| **4. Implement Negation Handling for Hinglish** <br> Explicitly check for and parse negative Hinglish descriptors (e.g., *"bina"* + verb/noun) during preprocessing to prevent false positive matches. | **Low to Moderate** <br> (+2.0% to +3.0% Top-3 Accuracy) | **Low to Medium** <br> (Adding regex rules or pre-processing steps) | **Low** <br> (Localized to pre-processing logic) | **Failure Case 16** ("bina baat kiye" matched speech) and **Failure Case 18** ("bina bole" matched speech) because the model matched the verb but ignored the negation "bina". |
| **5. Fine-Tune Retrieval Age Weighting** <br> Make the age decay curve smoother or domain-specific to avoid locking out overlapping milestone categories across boundary edges. | **Low** <br> (+1.0% Top-3 Accuracy) | **Low** <br> (Adjusting numerical constants in `calculate_age_weight`) | **Medium** <br> (Could lead to regression on edge ages) | **No direct failures** in the benchmark, but crucial for edge age-band queries in real-world environments. |
