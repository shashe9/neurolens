# Neurolens: 2-Minute Elevator Pitch

**Goal**: Deliver a high-impact, clinical-safety and evidence-focused pitch.

---

## 🎙️ Voiceover Script

"Hello, judges. We are Neurolens, and we are solving a critical bottleneck in early childhood developmental screening. 

Every year, millions of caregivers observe subtle behavioral developments in their children. But when they finally secure a pediatrician visit, they struggle to communicate this longitudinal context. Conversely, clinicians have less than 15 minutes per visit and lack organized, traceable evidence to guide developmental checks.

This communication gap leads to missed early-intervention windows.

**Neurolens is our solution.** It is an evidence-first developmental observation platform. It does **not** diagnose autism or output risky, black-box probability scores. Instead, it empowers caregivers to write observations in their own natural words—including local Hinglish terminology—and uses a local, privacy-preserving retrieval engine to map those observations directly to standard CDC and AAP milestones.

Caregivers retain complete control. They review, refine, and confirm milestone linkages. Once confirmed, Neurolens generates an **immutable, clinician-ready preparation report**. The pediatrician receives structured, chronological observations backed by source citations, designed to reduce clinician preparation effort and significantly improve the organization of parent-reported evidence.

By keeping the AI local and transparent, and keeping the diagnostic authority entirely in the hands of the clinician, Neurolens represents the future of responsible, evidence-based pediatric health care. 

Thank you."
